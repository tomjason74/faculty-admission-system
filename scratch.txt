The primary objective of MSIT 645 IT STRATEGIC LEADERSHIP AND STRATEGIC PLANNING COURSE is to ensure that key learning/insights gained by the students are significantly applied to their respective workplace/work unit that will contribute to improving systems, processes, and services to the stakeholders.The MSIT 645 IT STRATEGIC LEADERSHIP AND STRATEGIC PLANNING COURSErequires thorough research and consultation during its preparation. It is developed in a proactive process, facilitating discussions and agreement between the student and the university on its focus and strategy. It serves as a commitment between the student and the University to address the identified development concern of the unit/sector/organization or the partner community.
